Why Technicians Choose RepairXpert Over eMaint

  • Diagnosis, not asset tracking. eMaint tracks your equipment and schedules PMs. When a machine faults at 2 AM, it cannot tell you why or how to fix it. RepairXpert can, in under 5 seconds.
  • No per-user pricing tax. eMaint charges $69+ per user per month. Share RepairXpert across your entire team for one flat price. Five techs on eMaint costs $345/mo. Five techs on RepairXpert costs $19.99/mo.
  • Zero implementation overhead. eMaint requires weeks of setup, data migration, and training. RepairXpert works the moment you open it — enter a fault code or snap a photo and get your diagnosis instantly.
  • Works offline. RepairXpert uses a service worker for offline access to fault code lookups. No internet at the plant floor? No problem.

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Many teams use a CMMS like eMaint for asset tracking and work orders alongside RepairXpert for real-time fault diagnosis. RepairXpert fills the gap that eMaint does not cover: AI-powered troubleshooting when something actually breaks. At $19.99/mo, adding RepairXpert to your existing eMaint setup costs less than a single additional eMaint user.
