Why Technicians Choose RepairXpert Over Limble

  • AI that diagnoses, not just tracks. Limble tracks your maintenance work. RepairXpert uses AI to analyze fault codes, symptoms, and photos to tell you exactly what failed and how to fix it. These are fundamentally different tools.
  • No per-user pricing. Limble charges per user, which punishes you for giving more technicians access. RepairXpert is one flat price for your whole team.
  • Instant time-to-value. Limble requires configuring assets, locations, and workflows before it becomes useful. RepairXpert delivers a diagnosis the moment you enter a fault code or upload a photo.
  • MCP integration for AI workflows. RepairXpert includes an MCP server that lets AI assistants query fault codes and diagnostics programmatically. No other CMMS offers this.

Can You Use Both?

Absolutely. Limble handles your scheduled maintenance and work orders. RepairXpert handles the unplanned breakdowns when you need answers fast. At $19.99/mo, adding RepairXpert to your Limble setup costs less than a single additional Limble user and fills a capability gap that CMMS platforms do not address.
